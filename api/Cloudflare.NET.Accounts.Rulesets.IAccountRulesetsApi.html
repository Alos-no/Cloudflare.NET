Interface IAccountRulesetsApi

Namespace
Cloudflare.NET.Accounts.Rulesets
Assembly
Cloudflare.NET.dll

Defines the contract for managing Rulesets at the account level.

This includes WAF Custom Rules, Rate Limiting Rules, and other advanced configurations that apply across all zones in an account.
