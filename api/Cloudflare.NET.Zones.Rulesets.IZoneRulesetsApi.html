Interface IZoneRulesetsApi

Namespace
Cloudflare.NET.Zones.Rulesets
Assembly
Cloudflare.NET.dll

Defines the contract for managing Rulesets at the zone level.

This includes WAF Custom Rules, Rate Limiting Rules, Redirects, and other advanced configurations for a specific zone.
