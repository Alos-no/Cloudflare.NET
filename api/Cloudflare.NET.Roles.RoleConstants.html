Cloudflare.NET.Roles

Constants for Cloudflare account roles.

These are the standard role names returned by the Cloudflare API. Role availability varies by account type and subscription plan.

Remarks

Documentation: https://developers.cloudflare.com/fundamentals/manage-members/roles/

Not all roles are available on all account types. Free accounts may have access to a limited subset of roles. Use ListAccountRolesAsync(string, ListAccountRolesFilters?, CancellationToken) to discover which roles are available for a specific account.
